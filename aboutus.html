About

Established in the year 2000, Dilshan Auto Traders (Pvt) Ltd has journeyed through the years to become one of Sri Lanka's leading dealers and importers of all types of motor vehicles manufactured by some of the worlds highly renowned automakers such as Toyota, Honda, BMW and Land Rover.

Having imported thousands of automobiles ranging from personal to heavy vehicles, and with the experience of more than a decade, we are trusted by all for the quality of our motor vehicles and for our reputation as a customer-centric organisation dedicated towards providing superior customer service and after-sales care.

Purchasing a motor vehicle is one of the major decisions a person makes in his or her life. It often is a milestone event that is remembered and cherished for a long time. Becoming a vehicle owner is no longer only about finding a solution for a transportation requirement - it also is a matter relating to prestige, comfort, safety and economy. Your decision to purchase a motor vehicle must be backed by research and expert advice to ensure it is not just an expense, but an investment. We at Mahesh International are ever ready to assist you in choosing the right type of vehicle that fulfils your requirements beyond expectations.

We are well respected by our customers, stakeholder government and private institutions. We are proud to hold a long standing unspoilt reputation as a genuine vehicle trader - a title earned only by a deserving few in the automobile trade in Sri Lanka, one we are committed to guard zealously. A larger percentage of our vehicles are sold due to the recommendations and testimonials we receive by our clientele. We are proud to receive the patronage of our clients when it comes to all of their future vehicle purchases, a feat achieved through our unwavering commitment to our company mission statement - to "supply our customers with genuine high quality automobiles that fit their budgets and fulfils their expectations".

All vehicles are imported only after they have been thoroughly inspected to ensure they meet our organisations stringent quality, safety and performance standards. We only import vehicles that are in the highest band of the respective countries auction grade, have very low mileage and have been meticulously used by their first owners. We strictly do not import vehicles that have been damaged or have met with accidents. All vehicles in our showroom are in the exact paint, body and interior condition they were in when they were unloaded

ur staff are well trained and empowered to assist you with all your vehicle inquiries and to provide you with personal attention and the best service possible in selecting, preparing and delivering the right solution for your automotive need. They are continuously updated with the latest comfort, safety & technological features, accessories & trends available in the motor-world to help you with your selection. Once you have found your future auto-soul-mate, our staff would arrange for you a test-drive on request. Our showroom is open from 8 a.m. to 7.00 p.m. from Monday to Saturday and from 8 a.m. to 1 p.m. on Sundays.
